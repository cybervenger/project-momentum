The idea

Everyone sells you a plan.
Nobody asks about your life.

The Indian fitness internet is full of templates — the same six-day split, the same chicken-and-broccoli macros, sold to a hundred thousand people who have nothing in common except a credit card.

It fails for the obvious reason. A plan that doesn't know about your job, your commute, your mother's cooking, your bad knee, or the fact that you eat dinner at 10pm isn't a plan. It's a PDF.

We start with the person. Everything else is downstream of that.

Nutrition

Built from the kitchen you already have.

Your diet chart starts with what your house cooks — roti, rice, dal, paneer, eggs, whatever's actually on the table. We adjust portions, timing and balance. We don't hand you a shopping list from another country.

The best diet is the one your life can hold for a year, not the one that looks impressive for a fortnight.

Training

Three good days beat six loud ones.

Programs are written for the week you actually have — three or four sessions, gym or home, every set decided before you walk in so you never waste a session wondering what to do.

Strength first. Conditioning second. Nothing performed for a camera.

Accountability

Someone is actually watching.

Weekly check-ins where a person — not an app — looks at your weight, your lifts, your adherence, and tells you what to change. Plateaus get caught in week three, not month three.

This is the part every template skips, and it's the only part that reliably works.

The method

Four steps. No shortcuts in any of them.

01

We talk

A real conversation — your schedule, your food, your history, your injuries, what's failed before and why. Not a form.

02

We build

A diet chart and a training plan written for you specifically. Your kitchen, your week, your starting point.

03

You work

You execute. We stay reachable. Questions get answered by a person who remembers your case.

04

We adjust

Weekly review of what moved and what didn't. The plan changes as you change. That's the whole job.

Most fitness brands are a faceless account and an outsourced PDF. You'll know exactly who wrote your plan, because there are only two of us — and one of us will be on the call.
